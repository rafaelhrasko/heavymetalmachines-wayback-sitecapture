Play With Your Friends

You know that playing with friends is even better, right? You can carry the bomb while they kill enemies and provide your support, recovering your HP, or vice versa. So call your friends so you can play together!

If you’d like to meet more people who play Heavy Metal Machines, join our DISCORD server and meet an awesome crowd that loves sharing memes and setting up custom matches!

And whenever you feel ready, you can try playing the Ranked Mode!
